Pricing

Due to the nature of Furcation's venue we have a large variation in the number of accommodation types available and number of people that can share it. This does lead to some complexities in our pricing structures. However, below you will find a table displaying each of the accommodation options and the prices split by the capacity of each accommodation type. These prices are on a 'per person' basis, include the registration fee but do not include sponsor, early arrival or late departure.
