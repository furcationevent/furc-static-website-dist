Dealer's Den

This is where you can walk around and see what wares are being sold by our many dealers.

Unlike other conventions due to limited available space our den only operates for 3 hours on either Saturday or Sunday during the convention. One benefit of this is even as a dealer you still get to enjoy everything else Furcation has to offer.
