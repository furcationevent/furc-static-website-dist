Furcation Recruitment Role: First Aider

Download Furcation Recruitment Role: First Aider

Essential Requirements

  • Valid and in-date first aid certification (EFAW, FAW)
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Excellent organisational skills
  • Ability to remain calm under pressure

Desirable Requirements

  • Mental health first aid certificate

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Working with the wider first aid and medical team to provide response to first aid incidents
  • Recording incidents in line with team policies
  • Communication with colleagues via radio and instant messaging
  • Cleaning of treatment areas and equipment

Reports to

  • Welfare Manager
  • Medical / First Aid Head and Deputy Head of Department
  • Medical Staff

How to Apply

Please go to the support website and choose "recruitment" from the drop-down menu then fill out the application form adding in "First Aider"
