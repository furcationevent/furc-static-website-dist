Furcation Recruitment Role: Sponsor Coordinator

Download Furcation Recruitment Role: Sponsor Coordinator

Description

Furcation is looking to recruit a sponsor coordinator to helm the search and acquisition of goodies for our sponsor tier of attendance. This will involve working directly with the sponsor team to come up with enticing gifts for the sponsor pack. Finalising the sponsor pack and contacting suppliers to source items. This will need to be within a specific per head budget.

Furcation prides itself on involving its volunteer staff in many of the decisions underpinning the event. We have a friendly, inclusive, and welcoming internal culture which is built on an internal free exchange of ideas. In everything we do, we try our best to make sure the needs of our attendees come first.

Requirements

  • Effective communication skills
  • Ability to work to deadlines.

How to Apply

Please go to the support website and choose "recruitment" from the drop-down menu then fill out the application form adding in "Sponsor Coordinator"
